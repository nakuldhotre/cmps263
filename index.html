Gentrification: Property trends in New York Vs San Francisco

Rising property prices are pushing out people from cities. Gentrification usually affects old and poorer neighborhoods, this visualization will try to focus on such areas. We compare trends in New York and San Francisco on during a period of last 10 to 20 years. Here we compare property trends on various metrics such as home sizes(number of rooms and price per sqft), rent, rent to price ratio, etc.
